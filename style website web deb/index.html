Welcome to Ramp Strick Design Discord

Enhance your Discord server with our pre-formatted, easy-to-copy Discord-style text for your server categories like Rules, FAQ, and Server Design.

Rules

**1. Be Respectful**
    Treat everyone with respect. No harassment, hate speech, or discrimination.
    
**2. No Spam**
    Avoid spamming messages, images, or any other content.
    
**3. Use Appropriate Channels**
    Post content in the appropriate channels to keep the server organized.
    
**4. No NSFW Content**
    Sharing Not Safe For Work (NSFW) content is strictly prohibited.
    
**5. Follow Discord's Terms of Service**
    Ensure all activities comply with Discord's official Terms of Service.
    
**6. No Self-Promotion**
    Do not advertise your own or others' servers without permission.
    
**7. Protect Privacy**
    Do not share personal information of yourself or others.
    
**8. Listen to Staff**
    Respect the decisions and instructions of the server staff.
    
**9. Keep Content Legal**
    Do not share or discuss illegal activities or content.
    
**10. Have Fun**
    Engage positively and enjoy your time in the community.

FAQ

**Q1: How do I use the copied text?**
    A1: Simply paste the copied text into your Discord server channels to apply the formatting.
**Q2: Can I customize the text?**
    A2: Yes, feel free to modify the text to better suit your server's needs.
**Q3: How many rules can I add?**
    A3: You can add as many rules as you need to ensure your server runs smoothly.
**Q4: Is there a limit to server design templates?**
    A4: No, you can create and copy multiple server design templates to fit your server's theme.
**Q5: Do you offer custom templates?**
    A5: Currently, we provide standard templates, but custom templates may be available in the future.
